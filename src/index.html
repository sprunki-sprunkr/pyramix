Sprunki Pyramix

Sprunki Pyramix is an innovative music creation game that combines rhythm-based gameplay with engaging character customization. Set against a backdrop inspired by ancient Egyptian themes, players navigate through a mystical desert filled with towering pyramids and hidden treasures. The game features dynamic puzzle mechanics, allowing players to strategically align matching symbols and unlock hidden pathways while creating unique musical compositions.

How to Play Sprunki Pyramix

Begin your musical journey in ancient Egypt!

Getting Started

Launch Sprunki Pyramix on your browser, select a character and start your musical adventure.

Core Gameplay

Create music by dragging and dropping sound elements, customize your character with unlocked items, and master both Solo and Multiplayer modes for an enriching experience.

Advanced Techniques

Experiment with different sound combinations and time your additions carefully to create unique sound patterns. Master the rotating pyramid grids and cascading tiles for the ultimate musical experience.
